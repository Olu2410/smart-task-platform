About Teams
What are teams?

Teams are groups of users who collaborate on projects and tasks together.

Team Settings:
  • Public Team: Anyone can see the team and its members
  • Allow Self-Join: Users can join without an invitation
Team Roles:
  • Admin: Full control over the team
  • Manager: Can manage tasks and invite members
  • Member: Can work on tasks and view team content
  • Viewer: Read-only access to team content
