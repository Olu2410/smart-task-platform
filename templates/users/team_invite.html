Invitation Guide
How Invitations Work:
  • Invitations are sent via email
  • Invitations expire after 7 days
  • Users can accept or decline invitations
  • If the user doesn't have an account, they'll be prompted to create one
Role Permissions:
  • Admin: Full team control, can manage members and settings
  • Manager: Can create projects, manage tasks, and invite members
  • Member: Can work on tasks and view team content
  • Viewer: Read-only access to team content
Note: You can only invite users to roles equal to or below your current role.
